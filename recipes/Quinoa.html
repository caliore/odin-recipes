Quinoa Broccoli Salad by Gabrielle Brown aka @HealthyGirlKitchen

Description

This quinoa broccoli salad is easy to make, perfect for parties, to meal prep at the beginning of the week and is 100% vegan! This quinoa broccoli salad is hearty and nutritious to keep you fueled, while also being filling. If you are bored of the same old salad, mix it up and give this one a try! The vegan dill dressing is the showstopper of this dish to truly make it so delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa yields 4 cups when cooked
  • 1 cup finely chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup diced cucumbers
  • 2 cups diced roasted sweet potato
  • 3/4 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds

Steps

  1. Cook the quinoa and let it cool. You need 1 cup uncooked quinoa (yields about 4 cups cooked).
  2. Dice sweet potato into small cubes and roast in the oven in olive oil salt and pepper for 25 minutes or microwave in a microwave safe bowl for 5 minutes.
  3. Chop the cucumbers, and broccoli and prep the pomegranate seeds if you didn't buy them pre-shelled.
  4. Into a large bowl add the quinoa, sweet potato, cucumber, broccoli, almonds and pomegranate seeds. Pour the dressing over and mix to combine well.li>
  5. Store in a container in the fridge up to 5 days
